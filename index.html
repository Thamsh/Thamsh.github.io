About

Mohiddeen Thamsheer is a passionate writer, translator, and a dedicated Islamic scholar. His works reflect a deep engagement with contemporary Islamic thought and cultural memory. He is currently pursuing his Master's in Islamic Sciences at Madin Kulliya of Islamic Science College.

Research & Interests

Thamsheer’s research explores intersections of Islamic tradition with modern knowledge, education reform, spiritual practices, and Sufi heritage in South India. He seeks to build bridges between academic scholarship and community awareness through thoughtful writing and translation.
